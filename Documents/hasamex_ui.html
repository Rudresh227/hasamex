Core Features

Everything you need to
manage expert relationships

From onboarding a new expert to sending a shortlist to a client — every step of the workflow is covered.

Smart Search & Filters
Full-text search across name, title, bio, sector, and location. Filter by region, status, employment type, and function simultaneously.
Expert Profiles
Rich profiles with bio, employment history, strength topics, hourly rates, and direct LinkedIn integration. Attach profile PDFs with one click.
Email-Ready Export
Select multiple experts and generate a formatted HTML table ready to paste into Gmail, Outlook, or any email client. Also exports as CSV or Markdown.
Add & Edit Experts
Validated forms with real-time email and URL checking. Live duplicate detection warns you before saving if a LinkedIn URL or email already exists.
PDF Profile Attachments
Upload expert profile PDFs directly or attach a link. Files are stored in S3 and accessible via secure presigned URLs with configurable expiry.
Analytics Dashboard
At-a-glance stats on total experts, active T&C status, total calls completed, and average hourly rates across your network.
How It Works

From sourcing to shortlist
in four steps

01
Add or Import Experts
Add experts manually via the validated form, or bulk-seed from your existing Excel database. Duplicate detection runs automatically on email and LinkedIn URL.
02
Search & Filter
Use full-text search across all fields combined with Sector, Region, Status, and Employment filters to find the right expert in seconds.
03
Review Profiles
Open rich expert profiles with bio, employment history, strength topics, rates, and LinkedIn. Attach or download their profile PDF without leaving the app.
04
Export to Email
Select your shortlist and generate a formatted HTML table — copy it directly into any email client. Also available as CSV for CRM import.
